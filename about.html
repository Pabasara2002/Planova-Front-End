Our Story - Your Perfect Day

Founded with a simple belief that every celebration deserves to be extraordinary, Planova began as a small team of passionate event enthusiasts. Over the years, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted event planning companies, but our core values remain unchanged.

We believe in the power of attention to detail, the importance of listening to our clients' dreams, and the magic that happens when creativity meets flawless execution. Our journey has been marked by countless beautiful moments - from intimate weddings to grand corporate galas.

What sets us apart is our commitment to understanding that behind every event is a story waiting to be told. We don't just plan events; we craft experiences that become cherished memories for years to come.

Today, with a team of skilled professionals and a portfolio of successful events, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in event planning, always with the same passion that started it all.
